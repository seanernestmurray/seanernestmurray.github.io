About

My interests lie in cross-disciplinary quantitative research, applying mathematical and engineering concepts to fields of medicine, sociology, finance, and biology. Some of my projects and publications include Bayesian machine-learning approaches to improve radiation dose inference in nuclear medicine, applying dynamic network and graph theory concepts to evolving relations between social groups, and computational analysis of biological pattern formation.

I have recently recieved an MSc in Mathematical Modelling and Scientific Computing from the University of Oxford and have a BAI (Sch.) in Electronic and Computer Engineering from Trinity College Dublin.
